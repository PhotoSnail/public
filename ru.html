DIY 360° PRODUCT PHOTOGRAPHY AUTOMATIC TURNTABLE
for shooting Photo360 Videos and 3D-scanning
PhotoPizza - is open source project photography turntable based on Arduino controller. You can self-assemble the unit of available components, using detailed instructions, and you will not need soldering iron and a deep knowledge of electronics.

With the platform can sync any camera having a connector for wired remote control. All SLR cameras have this connector.

Many customizable rotation settings allow you to shoot objects in almost any light intensity. Compatible with software for remote camera control, such as EOS Utility from Canon or digiCamControl

With the help of these programs you can see in real time the photographed object on a computer screen and adjust the lighting scheme. Also, in the process of taking a picture is automatically saved on the computer.

The rate of development of the project depends on your help.

MAIN PROJECT IDEAS

Low cost
Our goal was to create a truly affordable solution, which would be expensive for many amateurs, and businesses of all sizes. As a result, the total cost of all the elements PhotoPizza significantly below any comparable characteristics, unique.


Availability components
We select the components so that you did not have major problems with their search and purchase. For example, the project uses bearings on inline skates, you can find the nearest sporting goods store.
Reliability construction and load capacity
Base load is not on the central axis and distributed uniformly over the support rollers the platform. Most of options have similar factory production platforms central axis is the "weak point" that limits the capacity. You do not have to cancel the shoot due to sudden mechanical failure. You can take pictures of people and objects massive enough.


Easy to assemble
Building construction will not take you much time and does not require specialized knowledge or complex tools. The control unit consists of electronic modules and easily interconnect with special conductors, and some are simply inserted into the slots.
Using different materials
Depending on the task, and the weight of the object being photographed, turntable parts can be made from materials raznh or even combine them.
Easy to transport
PhotoPizza lightweight, the largest platform can easily pick one person. Transporting and transferring FotoPitstsu can be piecemeal. You will be able to organize outreach shooting even when using public transport.


Versatility
